The Ten Commandments of Stock Trading

Dev Anand 10:49 AM Smart Investments

  1. You shall preserve your capital

  2. You shall stick to your stop-losses

  3. You shall not take bigger bets than you can afford to lose

  4. You shall not trade too many positions at the same time

  5. You shall not try to get rich quickly

  6. You shall never gamble on events

  7. You shall never average

  8. You shall never trade when you are low, unwell, sad, mad, or angry

  9. You shall spend time analyzing your trades, and not looking at the screen

  10. You shall take breaks, and not get addicted to gambling
